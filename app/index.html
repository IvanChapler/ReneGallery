Biography

Rene Magritte, born November 21, 1898, Belgium. one of the most prominent Surrealist painters, whose bizarre flights of fancy blended horror, peril, comedy, and mystery. His works were characterized by particular symbols-the female torso, the bourgeois "little man," the bowler hat, the apple,
the castle, the rock, the window.
